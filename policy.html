Privacy Policy

Effective January 1, 2026

ApexenLabs ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use this website, how it is used, and your rights — regardless of where you are in the world.

What Information I Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business or personal name (if provided)
  • Your project details or message
  • The plan you're interested in

This information is collected through the contact form on this site or through the booking system. We do not collect any data automatically, and there is no database storing your information on my end.

How Your Information Is Used

The information you submit is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Discuss and complete your project
  • Follow up via email, phone, or video call (such as Zoom) to present or review your website

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond completing your project.

Third-Party Services

This site uses a small number of third-party tools:

  • Cal.com — handles meeting bookings. When you book a meeting, your information is subject to Cal.com's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — loads fonts for the site and may set cookies as part of that delivery.

Beyond these, no other third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising tools are used on this site.

Cookies

This website does not intentionally use cookies. Google Fonts may set limited cookies as part of font delivery. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Your data is only seen by us and the third-party tools listed above.

International Users

This website is open to clients worldwide. If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may exercise these rights by contacting us directly.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only for as long as needed to complete your project. Once the project is done and there is no ongoing relationship, We do not keep your data on file.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information I hold about you. To do so, email neel@vermaclub.com and We will respond within 48 hours.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email neel@vermaclub.com.
