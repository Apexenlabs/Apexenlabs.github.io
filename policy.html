Privacy Policy

Effective January 1, 2026

ApexenLabs respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use this website, how it is used, and your rights.

We only collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Your name

This information is collected through the contact form or booking system. We do not collect any data automatically, and there is no database storing your information on my end.

The information you submit is used only to:

Your information is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing without your explicit consent.

Information submitted through the contact form is retained only as long as needed to complete your project. You may request deletion at any time by emailing apexenlabs@gmail.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information you've provided. Contact me directly and I'll respond within 5 business days.

Either party may cancel a project before work is completed. If I have already begun work, a partial payment may be requested for work completed. If you cancel before I start, no payment is due.

Refund Policy

If you cancel before I start on your project, no payment is due and no refund is needed.

If you're unhappy with the direction of the project at any point, let me know immediately. I'll work with you to get it right through revisions. The earlier you raise concerns, the easier it is to fix them.

If the final delivered website does not match what was agreed upon in our initial discussion, I will make it right with revisions at no additional cost.

A full or partial refund may be issued in the following situations:
